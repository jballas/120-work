Fairy Doctor Runs Away

Story

Lillia has gotten herself into a situation. She used to be a fairy doctor, but had to quit for various reason. Her marriage was a mistake. She's desperate to escape. Her only choice? Run away. Unfortunately, the fairies have this crazy idea that Lillia needs to be their fairy doctor. She doesn't, but they just don't understand. What's a girl to do?

Controls

About

Fairy Doctor Runs Away is a p5.js game inspired by the fantasy series Fairy Doctor Falls in Love by Ava Clary. It is aso the final project for my Media Arts Creative Coding 1 at the University of Montana. Veiw my code at github. Find out more about Ava Clary on her website.

Thank you for playing!
